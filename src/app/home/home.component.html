Privacy Policy

Shnell is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and drivers. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data:

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, contact information, location, and other details to provide you with a seamless experience when using our app. This information is used to connect you with drivers and for other necessary logistics operations.

2. Data Usage

Your personal data is primarily used to ensure a smooth and efficient service. We may use this data for customer support, to resolve disputes, or to improve our service. Information about drivers may also be used in legal cases, should an issue arise between a user and a driver.

3. Responsibility for Goods

Shnell takes no responsibility for any goods that are accidentally lost, stolen, or damaged during the delivery process. However, we will assist customers by providing information about the driver involved based on our collected data, which may be used in legal proceedings. Shnell itself is not liable for any incidents involving the goods.

4. Driver and User Agreement on Price

If the initial price proposed by the app is not suitable for both the driver and the user, they are free to agree upon another price. However, regardless of any changes in the agreed price, the app will still take the commission based on the original proposed price.

5. Rights of Users and Drivers

Both users and drivers have the right to access, modify, and delete their personal data at any time. Drivers and users are also responsible for ensuring that they comply with local regulations while using our platform. Shnell may suspend or terminate access to the app if we detect any misuse.

6. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data. However, in case of any breach or unauthorized access, we will notify affected users immediately and take appropriate action.

7. Commission Fees

Shnell takes a commission on each transaction as proposed by the app. This commission is fixed based on the initial price, regardless of any other agreement made between the driver and the user.

By using the Shnell platform, you agree to these policies and acknowledge that you have read and understood the terms. If you have any concerns or require further information, please contact us through our support channels.
